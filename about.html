KNOW MORE




"Give a Hand" is a registered Non-Profit Company that is based in Kakinada – Andhra Pradesh. We are committed to community social welfare, children education,focusing on disadvantaged communities and individuals within Kakinada and near poverty areas.

Our Mission :
  • Focus on basic education to the poor children
  • Work towards giving basic needs to the children
  • Make children aware of the current technology
  • Create an environment where the abandoned children can stay
  • Give access to quality education for the poor children
  • Make a team of volunteers to take care of the children.
Our Vision:
  • Prepare for emergencies by identifying, mitigating and managing risks
  • Prevent emergencies and support development of tools necessary during outbreaks
  • Detect and respond to acute health emergencies
  • Support delivery of essential health services in fragile settings
Our Values :
  • Right to education is the basic right
  • Providing quality education
  • Make sure no children left abandoned
Our Working :
  • Creating a team of volunteers to take care of children
  • Making educational campaigns in a backward areas
  • Creating awareness about education among poor people
  • Providing basic daily goods to the backward families
  • Working on creating an environment for the abandoned children
  • Elimination and eradication of illiteracy in future generations.
